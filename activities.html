Only the most devoted (or foolhardy) acolytes delve into these specialized practices. Mr. Battista takes a *personal* interest in students excelling in these areas. His fangs... I mean, his favor, is a great reward.

Midnight Feasting Rituals

Gather 'round the blood-stained altar as we partake in... unique delicacies. Learn the proper etiquette for draining your 'donations' and pleasing the palate of true connoisseurs like Mr. Battista. He demands only the freshest offerings.

MR. BATTISTA'S DECREE: The next Blood Moon Festival requires a special 'contribution' from ALL attendees. Ensure your veins are full and your will is weak. Your host, Mr. Battista, is particularly thirsty.

Advanced Soul-Binding

Learn to tether lesser spirits to your will, or perhaps, bind your own soul to an eternity of servitude to a greater power. Mr. Battista himself is a master of this, and his collection of bound souls is... impressive. He's always looking for new additions.

Tread carefully, for these paths are fraught with peril and eternal consequences. But the power... oh, the power is intoxicating.
